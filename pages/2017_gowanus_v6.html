another waterfront performing arts center to revitalize Gowanus
The Gowanus Performing Art Center makes use of sectional changes to calibrate over-under spatial sequences from the point of entry on site to the upper gallery and concert hall spaces. The interweaving of experimental and traditional forms of media carries visitors through a spectrum of experiences, ranging from viewing a contemporary artist’s workshop to attending a classical orchestral concert. To address conditions of the ‘local’, the building is simultaneously a destination for cultural activities, a familiar refuge for Brooklyn residents, and a terminus for the waterfront.
