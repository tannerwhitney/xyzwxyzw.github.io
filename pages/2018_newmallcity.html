NEWMALLCITY


In the translation of abstracted landscape studies into spatial elements, scale shifts provided room for confusion of type, material, and function. This proved advantageous on a massive site, allowing for simultaneous consideration of sculptural building-scale and intimate human-scale moves. Ideas of Victor Grün (pioneer of the suburban mall) were a point of entry into designing for the modern flaneur in Shanghai, where colossal proportions might seem antithetical to hand-hewn specificity.
